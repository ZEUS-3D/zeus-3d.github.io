announcements (as of dec. 7, 2012)

The exams are graded, though I'm still awaiting grades for assignment 10 and the labs before I can assess final course grades and submit them to the registrar. You are welcome to stop by to review your exam, if you wish.

Hardcover copies of edition 7 of the PHYS 2301 text, Analytical Mechanics by Fowles and Cassiday, are available in the bookstore for about $180, or you might still try to order it on-line (e.g., amazon.ca). Either edition 6 or 7 are perfectly fine.

If you'd like to get a "head-start" in PHYS 2301 over the break, here's an "early-bird" tip: I'll be treating chapters 1 and 2 in F&C as a review of PHYS 1100 (you can download my summary here), and I'll be assigning a homework set from these chapters on the first day of classes. I will post this set on the PHYS 2301 home page before classes start, in case any of you want to get started on it sooner.

Have a well-deserved holiday break, everyone, and I'll see most of you in PHYS 2301 in January!
