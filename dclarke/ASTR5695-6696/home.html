Following is a schedule for Journal Club presentations for the fall, 2004.
All sessions meet in MM310 on Fridays, 10:00am - 11:00am
In `shotgun' format (through November 12), everyone comes prepared to discuss the paper, and someone's
name is drawn to lead the discussion.
In `pick and pontificate' format (last three weeks), each speaker has 15-20 minutes (about eight viewgraphs)
for his or her presentation, leaving ten minutes for discussion.
