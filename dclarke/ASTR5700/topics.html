ASTR 5700 Special Topics

Students need to choose a special topic by October 6, 2010 and be prepared to give a 30-50 minute presentation on it some time during exam week. Your presentation should be accompanied by a LaTeX document suitable to be added to the MHD text as a chapter or part of a chapter in Part II. For "bonus points", you might even include one or two "problems" related to your topic in keeping with the end-of-chapter problems in Part I of the on-line text.

Suggested topics are included below. You may, of course, come up with your own topic and, if you do, please consult with me before October 6 when your topic selection is due.

Suggested Topics

1. Resistive MHD and ambipolar diffusion.

2. MHD Instabilities: MHD Kelvin-Helmholtz and/or Rayleigh-Taylor.

3. MHD Instabilities: Parker, Tearing, and/or Magnetorotational.

4. Radiation hydrodynamics and the radiative transfer equation.

5. The Boltzmann Equation.

6. Relativistic (M)HD.
