announcements (as of dec. 15, 2008)

Final grades have been assessed and submitted.  Average exam grade was 48%, average final grade was 54%.  There were 21 As, 8 Bs, 15 Cs, 22 Ds, and 28 Fs.  Congratulations to this year's honour rolls:

High honour roll (90% or better):  Alanna Durand, Alexander Mott, Kenzie Phillips, and Damien Robertson.

Honour Roll (80% - 89%): Wilfried Beslin, Tyler Cuthbert, Jane Ferguson, Kendra Fulford, Jacques Gamache, Brandon Hawes, Rebecca Lynch, Ian MacLeod, Jeffery Mayfield, Matthew McMullin, Kyle Peace, James Prudhoe, Jenna Richardson-Prager, Kendall Thompson, Gregory Tomney, Bradley Vickers, and Andrew Weseen.

Students who registered for both PHYS 1100 and 1101 by September 2008 and did not pass PHYS 1100 will be deregistered for PHYS 1101 by the Registrar.  For these students, there are two possibilities:

  1. Students whose PHYS 1100 grade was between 40% and 49% are eligible for "Clarke's Clemency Clause":
    • I will sign a prerequisite waiver to allow you into PHYS 1101.
    • If your PHYS 1101 grade is between 70% -- 79%, your F in PHYS 1100 will be changed to a D.  If you get 80% or better, your F (or D) will be changed to a C-!

        

  2. Those students with < 40% in PHYS 1100 and those students whose F in PHYS 1100 is not bumped to a pass by "ClClCl" must retake PHYS 1100.  If there is sufficient demand, the department may offer both PHYS 1100 and 1101 during the summer of 2009.  The more students who tell Dr. Short (ishort "at" zeus3d.ca; chair of our department) they need PHYS 1100/1101 during the summer of 2009, the more likely they will be offered then.

Clarke's Clemency Clause is not automatic.  Students who failed PHYS 1100 and who wish to attempt PHYS 1101 must talk to me (dclarke "at" zeus3d.ca) first so I can give you the necessary waiver to enrol in PHYS 1101.
